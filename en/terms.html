2. What Is Auto-Renewal

Auto-renewal is a subscription mechanism that allows your OpenFlat paid service to automatically renew at the end of each billing period.

If you enable auto-renewal and do not cancel it before the renewal date, you authorize OpenFlat (or the applicable payment provider) to charge the subscription fee for the next billing cycle and extend your service validity accordingly.

3. Billing Cycles

Available billing cycles may include:

The specific billing cycle and price applicable to you are shown on the purchase page at the time of subscription.

4. Automatic Charging Rules

4.1 Authorization to Charge

By enabling auto-renewal, you authorize OpenFlat and/or the relevant payment service provider to:

  • Automatically charge the subscription fee for the next billing cycle;
  • Deduct the applicable amount from your selected payment method;
  • Do so without requiring additional password, SMS verification, or repeated confirmation (unless required by law or the payment provider).

4.2 Third-Party Payment Methods

Auto-renewal charges depend on the payment method you selected:

(1) WeChat Pay / Alipay / Bank Card / Other Online Payment Methods

  • The charge will be attempted on the subscription expiration date.
  • If the payment fails (e.g., insufficient balance, expired card, payment limits), we may retry within a reasonable period.
  • If payment continues to fail, auto-renewal will be terminated and your service will expire at the end of the current billing period.

(2) Apple In-App Purchase (IAP)

  • If you subscribed through Apple, billing and renewal are governed by Apple’s subscription rules.
  • Apple will charge your Apple ID account on the renewal date.
  • If payment fails, Apple may enter its official retry period.
  • If payment is not successfully completed during Apple’s retry period, the subscription will lapse.

To manage Apple subscriptions:

iOS Device → Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → OpenFlat → Cancel Subscription

5. Cancellation of Auto-Renewal

You may cancel auto-renewal at any time.

5.1 Cancellation via OpenFlat

You may cancel through:

  • Log in to OpenFlat official website
  • Go to Account Center
  • Select Subscription Management
  • Click Manage Auto-Renewal
  • Choose Cancel Auto-Renewal

Once canceled, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period and will not renew thereafter.

5.2 Apple Subscription Cancellation

If subscribed via Apple IAP, cancellation must be completed through Apple’s system:

iOS Device → Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → OpenFlat → Cancel

5.3 Cancellation Timing

  • Changes to auto-renewal status may not be immediate.
  • Any renewal instruction already submitted before cancellation becomes effective may still be processed.
  • Fees already charged are generally non-refundable unless otherwise required by applicable law.
